About PointProjector

What is it?

PointProjector is a deformer object that non-destructively projects the points of splines and polygon objects ontp a geometry.

Installation

If you have already an older version of PointProjector installed (maybe even still called "SplineProjector"), remove its directory.

Just unzip the archive and place the folder "PointProjector" inside the folder "Cinema 4D Rxx/plugins".

When you start CINEMA 4D the next time, PointProjector will be available from the Plugins menu.

Attributes

PointProjector's attributes and what they do:

Projector

All attributes used for the point projection are found here.

Link

Link the geometry you want to project the points on here.

Mode

Select the projection mode here:

Parallel

Points are projected along the direction of the PointProjector's Z axis.

Good for projecting roads on landscapes, blankets on the ground, et cetera.

Spherical

Points are projected away from the PointProjector's position.

Good for projecting stuff inside cavities or concave objects.

Offset

Move the projected points along the face normal of the geometry they've been projected on.

Blend

Seamlessly blend between projected and unprojected shape.

Geometry Falloff

Enables the geometry falloff mode.

In geometry falloff mode, points are projected only within a certain range, starting from the PointProjector object. Within that range, points are moved towards the surface using a soft step interpolation.

Distance

Defines the size of the falloff area around the PointProjector.
